All Problems Are Hard Aren’t all problems hard? Say a customer reports a bug, and you’ve got to fix it. You can fix it. You know you can fix it. Maybe it’ll take an hour, maybe it’ll take a day, maybe a week. But you’ll narrow it down, figure out the smallest possible increment of work you can do to remedy the problem, and then you’ll do it. And as much as it pains me to say it, because I want to be able to check that box that said I did the thing… this is the easy part.
